About the Band

We are the Yielele Band. We are 2 women and 2 men who got together in 2010 to make music. We are passionate about making music that makes you get up off the couch or the bar stool or the matatu seat and dance like nobody's watching. (Don't do the matatu thing, maybe just be here for a long time too if you can help it.) Anyway, we are very excited to meet you online, at our concerts around and outside Nairobi, most of all at Raha Resort where we play every last Friday of the month.
